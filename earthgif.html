Earth is our home planet and the third planet from the Sun. Its unique in the solar system because its the only known planet to support life. Heres a detailed description: Size and Structure: Earth is the fifth largest planet in terms of size and mass in our solar system. It has a mean radius of about 6,371 kilometers and is shaped into an oblate spheroid, meaning its slightly flattened at the poles and bulging at the equator1. Surface: The Earths surface is approximately 70.8% water, mostly in the form of oceans, and 29.2% land, which includes continents and islands1. The land features mountains, valleys, and plains, and is subject to processes like erosion and tectonic activity.
